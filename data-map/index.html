Introduction
This project visualizes my personal tea consumption along many other factors and data that can be found behind it. The map features various countries that my tea comes from and includes the farmers and their stories behind it. A majority of my tea is purchased through Adagio Teas, a company that highlights their direct involvement with their tea source to raise the living and working standards of growers.
